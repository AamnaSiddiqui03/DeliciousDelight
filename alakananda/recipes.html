Chole bhature is a popular North Indian dish made with spicy chickpeas (chole) and deep-fried bread (bhature)

Curd Rice

Ramen

Alu Paratha

Aloo paratha is a popular Indian flatbread stuffed with spiced mashed potatoes and cooked on a griddle with ghee or oil, often served with yogurt or pickle.

Mushroom Manchurian is an Indo-Chinese dish made with deep-fried mushroom balls coated in a spicy, tangy sauce, often served as an appetizer.
