Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Cheese
  • Veggies
  • Seasonings and herbs

Instructions:

  1. Make the pizza dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, yeast, salt, sugar, and water. Mix until the dough forms a ball. Knead the dough for 5-10 minutes until it is smooth and elastic. Cover and let the dough rise for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  2. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  3. Roll out the dough: Dust your work surface with flour and roll out the dough to the desired thickness and shape.
  4. Add toppings: Spread tomato sauce on the dough and add your desired toppings, such as cheese, vegetables, and meats.
  5. Bake the pizza: Place the pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone and bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Slice and serve: Remove the pizza from the oven, slice it into pieces, and serve hot.
